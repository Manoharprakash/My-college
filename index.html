About Us

Mahendra Institute of Technology (MIT) is a private engineering college located in Namakkal district of Tamil Nadu,
India. It was established in 2007 and is affiliated with Anna University, Chennai. The college offers undergraduate and
postgraduate courses in engineering and technology, including Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication
Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Civil Engineering.
